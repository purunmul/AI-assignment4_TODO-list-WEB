About Focus-Todo

Why We Built This

Focus-Todo was created for college students who need a fast, simple, and intuitive tool to record and check off daily tasks. We found that many planner applications are too heavy or complicated for quick daily use, leading to more procrastination. This service provides a distraction-free environment where you can intuitively manage study goals and tasks within seconds.

Quick User Guide

Add a Task: Go to the Dashboard, type your task into the input field, and press 'Enter' or click the 'Add Task' button.

Complete a Task: Click the checkbox next to a task. It will be visually marked as complete with a strikethrough.

Delete a Task: Click the 'Delete' button (trash icon) next to the task you wish to remove.

Your tasks are automatically saved in your browser, so they'll be here when you return!
